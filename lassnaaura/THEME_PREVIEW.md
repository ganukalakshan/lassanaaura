## ğŸ¨ Theme Features

### Gradients
- **Header:** 135deg pink to darker pink
- **Buttons:** 135deg pink gradient
- **Icons:** Pink gradient backgrounds
- **Cards:** White to light pink subtle gradient
- **Sidebar:** 180deg white to light pink

### Shadows
- **Small:** Subtle depth for inputs
- **Medium:** Cards and containers
- **Large:** Sidebar and modals
- **XL:** Welcome/login pages

### Hover Effects
- **Lift Animation:** translateY(-2px to -4px)
- **Shadow Increase:** Deeper on hover
- **Color Change:** Pink background on links
- **Transform:** Smooth 0.3s ease transitions

### Icons
- **Font Awesome 6.4.0:** All icons
- **Solid Style:** fa-solid / fas
- **Sizes:** Responsive, contextual
- **Colors:** Inherits from parent or themed

### Typography
- **Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Weights:** 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800
- **Sizes:** Responsive, hierarchical
- **Colors:** Primary (#333) and Secondary (#666)
